Step 1:

  • Get pizza dough
  • Kneed dough, add flour ass needed
  • Roll out with rolling pin
  • Flatten out into large thin circle
  • Put on pizza stone

Step 2:

  • Get Pot
  • Add 1 cup olive oil
  • Add 10 cloves of chopped garlic, simmer
  • Add 28oz whole peeled tomatoes
  • Add 28oz crushed tomotoes
  • Add 1 cup of water
  • Add basil sprig
  • Bring to boil, simmer 30min
  • Remove basil, Let rest 10min
  • Spread thin layer over rolled out dough

Step 3:

  • Get block of mozzarella cheese
  • Shred on small side of box grater
  • Spread cheese on top of sauce
  • Bake in oven 450degrees for 8min
